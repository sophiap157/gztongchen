Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with us.

We work on a wide range of digital products including mobile apps (iOS and Android), generative audio tools, immersive design systems, privacy-first platforms, and custom software solutions. Our sweet spot is in the intersection of technology innovation and user experience—projects that push boundaries while remaining practical.

We offer three engagement models: fixed-price for well-defined projects, monthly retainers for ongoing work, and custom enterprise agreements. Every project starts with a free consultation to understand your needs and provide a tailored proposal. We are transparent about costs and always provide detailed estimates before any work begins.

Timelines vary based on project complexity. A focused MVP can launch in 4-8 weeks, while comprehensive products typically take 3-6 months. We work in short iterative cycles and ship working software early and often. You'll see progress every week and can provide feedback continuously.

Absolutely. We routinely sign mutual NDAs before discussing project details. We take intellectual property protection seriously and have standard processes in place. Just let us know your NDA requirements at the start of our conversation.

Privacy-by-design is foundational to our work. We use local-first architectures, end-to-end encryption for any cloud sync, and minimize data collection to only what's necessary. We can help you navigate GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, and other privacy regulations while building user trust.
