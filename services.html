Deep expertise in generative audio and music technology—where code meets creativity.

Privacy Engineering

Build trust with your users through privacy-respecting technology and architecture.

End-to-End Encryption

Zero-knowledge architectures where even we cannot access user data. Industry-standard cryptographic protocols implemented correctly.

Local-First Storage

Architectures that prioritize local data. Cloud sync is opt-in, encrypted, and minimal—we never store what we don't need.

Privacy Compliance

GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, and global privacy regulations. We help you meet legal requirements while building user trust.

Audit & Assessment

Comprehensive privacy audits of existing products. We identify risks, recommend improvements, and help you implement them.

Differential Privacy

Aggregate insights without exposing individual data. Modern privacy-preserving analytics for the post-cookie era.

Zero-Trust Architecture

Modern security models that assume no implicit trust. Identity verification, least-privilege access, and continuous validation.
